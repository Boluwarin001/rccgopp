🔥 Youth Events

Every year, we gather together as youths on a retreat to immerse ourselves in the pursuit and presence of The Holy Spirit, to learn about Jesus and to be equipped to live the God-life.

We also play games and have fun. This year’s youth retreat is on the 10th - 14th of August. We also host 3 conferences every year called “Alone with God”. Come, taste and see that the Lord is good.

×
🤝 Homeless Outreaches

Every last Saturday of the month, the outreach team walks around Piccadilly handing out hot drinks and food to various homeless individuals on the street. Not only do we feed them, but we pray for them and spread the message of the gospel with them.

🌿 Branching Out

We are a small church with a large heart for Manchester. We know we are one slice of a major movement that God is doing around the city and across the country. We help each other in connecting with the larger projects in our parent church and our community. From logistics to “Festival of Life” events to volunteering in “Let’s Go A-Fishing”, we are committed to the work that God is doing through His servant, Pastor E. A. Adeboye.
