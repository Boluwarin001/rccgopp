Welcome Home,

Dearly beloved, In these last days, the bible says that wickedness will multiply, and the love of many will grow cold. We are called to be the voice of the voiceless and the hope of the hopeless. We are called to be the hands and feet of Jesus Christ.



It is my sincere prayer and hope for you dear beloved, to seek the face of God and keep extra oil. We must repent and believe the gospel, and times of refreshing as the Word says, will come upon you. We are here to pray and do life with you as a family. We love you, and our Lord Jesus Christ loves you.



Pastors Oluyemi & Aderike Coker.
