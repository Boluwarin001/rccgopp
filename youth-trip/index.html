Day One

We left Manchester at 12:01pm after waiting for several hours because certain people (who shall not be named) decided that the meeting time was just a suggestion. We arrived at Kidderminster at 3pm and got settled in. After taking the safety drill, the onboarding process began. Bags were handed out, and people were allocated to their rooms. We had our first dinner and got settled in with the first major event of the day.

Day Two

The day began super early with the prayer walk (more of these to come). We went on the sky dangling and wall climbing thing (The writer of this piece didn't bother to learn the real name because if Our Lord wanted him to fly, He would have made him an airplane). We had a great session with Mr. Joel O from Joel O Cleans who gave an inspiring session on vision, finances and following the Holy Spirit (his "senior partner") in business. Finally the day ended with the movie - "Hacksaw Ridge". After the movie, several people rededicated their lives to Jesus and many stayed back to seek Him in prayer.

Day Three

Time to wake up! After the prayer walk and breakfast, our first session was with our Pastor who spoke to us about Marriage, Dating and Relationships. After, Mama Rose delved into the world of politics and educated us on the world of government and the Christian's role in it.

Day Four

Day 4 started with Derick going through morning exercies for the entire group. This was followed by a morning charge from Pastor who spoke to us about the emergency visitations of God. After getting ready and having our breakfast, we welcomed our guests, from Godly Youth. Minister Favour gave a word regarding the glory. This was followed by a time of prayer for the Godly Youths team, where words of prophecy regarding rebirth was given. After lunch, we had another guest- Minister Ituni Ojo who expounded on the glory that comes from faith in God (to the schools and workplace).

And that's a warp!
